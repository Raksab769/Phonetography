Connect Your Instagram

Link your Instagram account once — your photos will automatically appear here every time someone visits your site. No manual updates ever needed.

1
Create a free Behold account
Go to behold.so and sign up free. No credit card needed.
→ Open Behold.so ↗
2
Connect your Instagram
In Behold → click Add Source → choose Basic → log in with Instagram. No developer account needed!
3
Create a Feed & copy the Feed ID
In Behold → click Add Feed → select your Instagram source → choose JSON Feed → copy the Feed ID (looks like: AbCd1234)
4
Paste the Feed ID below
That's it! Your photos will automatically appear on your website.
